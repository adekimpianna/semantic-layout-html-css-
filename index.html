Orang Utan

Orang Utan: Critically Endangered

It is common knowledge that a large number of different animals are currently threatened with extinction. Orang utan is one of them! Based on the recent list of threatened species issued by WFF and National Geographic, the existence status of this ape has gotten worse from Endangered (per 1950) to Critically Endangered (per 2016) mainly due to the loss of rain forest. If the number of this species keep decreasing, there is a big unfortunate possibility that Orang Utan will not survive another 50 years.
